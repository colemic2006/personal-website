About Me

I’ve been in the security industry for over 25 years and have lived and worked all around the world supporting different programs and projects as an Airman, a DoD contractor, a security analyst, firewall engineer, security architect, CISO, and executive consultant, and everything in between. Based in Abilene, Texas, I specialize in working with SMBs and MSPs (and enterprises, too) to build and refine security programs using established frameworks. Outside of work, I’m a husband, father, and Texas High School football official. When I’m not in the office, you can find me reffing, golfing, tinkering in my garage, or reading about new technologies.
